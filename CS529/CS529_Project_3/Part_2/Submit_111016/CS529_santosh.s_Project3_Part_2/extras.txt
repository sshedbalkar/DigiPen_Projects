Extras:
1. Particle effect for forward acceleration
2. Particle effect for bullet fire
3. Random generation of asteroid shapes (Asteroid shapes are generated randomly)
4. Blinking circles (red/green) around the ship when you die/level-up


Known issues:
1. Animated point to static circle collision detection is imperfect so there are issues with the functionalities that use this function, namely: Asteroid-Asteroid collision, Bullet-Asteroid collision, Ship-Asteroid collision
